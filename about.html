Our story: 14 years of care

For over 14 years, Revive Dental Clinic has been a pillar of oral health and aesthetic excellence in Pune. Located in Pride World City, Charholi Budruk, we have transformed the smiles and lives of over 3,500 patients.

What started as a vision for better dental care has grown into a legacy of trust. We have stood by our patients through every milestone — from a child’s first tooth to life-changing full-mouth rehabilitations. Our longevity is built on a simple premise: treating every patient like family while using the most advanced dental technologies available.

Our mission

To deliver exceptional, pain-free dental care that empowers our patients with lasting oral health and radiant, confident smiles.

Our vision

To be recognized as Pune’s premier dental care destination, setting global standards for clinical excellence, patient comfort, and digital innovation.

Our core values

How we show up for you every day.

Empathy

We listen, we care, and we design treatments around your comfort levels and needs. Fear is often the biggest barrier to health — we work to dissolve it.

Excellence

Innovation

Digital dentistry and modern diagnostics — for precision, speed, and ease.

Our founder and principal dentist

Dr. Mrudul Salunke founded Revive Dental Clinic and leads clinical care with a calm, patient-first approach.

Dr. Mrudul Salunke

Founder and Principal Dentist, Revive Dental Clinic, Town Plaza, Pride World City, Pune. Dr. Mrudul Salunke is known for delivering advanced dental treatment in a calm, soothing environment where patients feel confident and cared for from day one.

BDS, Nair Hospital Dental College, Mumbai (2013). She has a special interest in Endodontics and Cosmetic Dentistry, and has completed training in surgical and prosthetic implantology, modern endodontics, practice management, and soft skills under renowned dentists in India.

Her clinical work spans General and Cosmetic Dentistry, Crown and Bridge work, Tooth Whitening, and Endodontics, with continuous upskilling through hands-on workshops in dental emergencies, Basic Life Support, stress management, and dental photography.
