# Page 3: Treatments & Procedures
*(This section is designed to be comprehensive, showing expertise across all dental disciplines.)*

**Headline:** Comprehensive Care for Every Stage of Life

### 1. Preventive & General Dentistry
The foundation of a lifetime of healthy smiles.
* **Comprehensive Oral Exams & Digital X-Rays:** Catching issues before they become problems.
* **Professional Teeth Cleaning (Scaling & Polishing):** Removing stubborn plaque and tartar for a fresh, healthy mouth.
* **Cavity Fillings:** Tooth-colored, durable composite fillings that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth.
* **Pediatric Dentistry:** Gentle, anxiety-free care tailored specifically for children's developing smiles.

### 2. Cosmetic Dentistry & Smile Designing
Unlock the smile you’ve always dreamed of.
* **Digital Smile Design (DSD):** Using digital imaging to map out and preview your perfect smile before treatment begins.
* **Teeth Whitening:** Professional, medical-grade whitening for a dazzling smile in under an hour.
* **Porcelain Veneers & Laminates:** Ultra-thin custom shells to correct chips, gaps, and severe discoloration.

### 3. Restorative Dentistry
Rebuilding form, function, and aesthetics.
* **Root Canal Treatment (RCT):** Advanced, pain-free endodontics designed to save infected teeth, often completed in a single visit.
* **Dental Crowns & Bridges:** High-strength Zirconia and ceramic restorations to replace missing or damaged teeth.
* **Dentures (Complete & Partial):** Comfortable, custom-fitted removable replacements for missing teeth.

### 4. Orthodontics
Straightening teeth for optimal health and appearance.
* **Traditional & Ceramic Braces:** Highly effective alignment for complex dental corrections.
* **Clear Aligners (Invisible Braces):** The modern, virtually invisible way to straighten your teeth without wires or brackets.

### 5. Oral Surgery & Dental Implants
Advanced solutions for complex needs.
* **Dental Implants:** The gold standard for tooth replacement. Titanium posts that fuse with your jawbone for a permanent, natural feel.
* **Wisdom Tooth Extraction:** Safe, comfortable surgical removal of impacted or problematic wisdom teeth.
* **Bone Grafting & Sinus Lifts:** Preparing the jawbone to successfully support dental implants.
